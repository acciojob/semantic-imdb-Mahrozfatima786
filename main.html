Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. He is offered a chance to have his past crimes forgiven if he can implant an idea into someone's mind, a process known as inception.
