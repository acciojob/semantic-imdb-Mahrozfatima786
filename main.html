Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully implant an idea into someone's mind, a process known as inception.
