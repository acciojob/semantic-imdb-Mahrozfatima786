Summary

Inception is a mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, enters people's dreams to steal secrets. He is offered a chance to have his past crimes forgiven if he can successfully perform inception—planting an idea in someone's mind.
